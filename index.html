Introdution

Rwanda Youth Impact believes that change is possible. Rwanda Youth Impact is a local non-governmental organization that works to protect in a non-discriminating manner the society, youth and children from harmful habits in consideration of human dignity and esteem. We believe that when children and youth are empowered and exposed to good moral values, we are assured of a brighter future for the nation.

Our Fields of Activity

Having commenced its activities against alcohol addiction at its foundation in 1920, Green Crescent has expanded its activities so as to cover other types of addictions over the course of its development hitherto.

Drug & Alcohol Addiction


A chronic, relapsing disorder characterized by compulsive drug seeking, continued use despite harmful consequences, and long-lasting changes in the brain.

Technology Addiction


Technology addiction is an impulse control disorder that involves the obsessive use of mobile devices, the internet despite negative consequences to the user.

Gambling Addiction


Compulsive gambling is the uncontrollable urge to keep gambling despite the toll it takes on your life. You will be willing to risk something you value in the hope of getting something of even greater value.

Tobacco Addiction


Nicotine dependence is a chronic, relapsing disease defined as a compulsive craving to use the drug, despite harmful social consequences
